📋 Expected File Format

Each row represents one evidence recommendation. Columns marked Required must be present. Filter columns use pipe-separated values (e.g. low|medium) — leave blank or use * to match all values.

Recommendation ID
Unique identifier for the recommendation (e.g. CL-001)
HTA domain
Top-level HTA domain (e.g. Clinical Effectiveness)
HTA dimension
Sub-dimension within the domain (e.g. Comparative effectiveness)
Overarching Evidence Recommendation
Short, overarching recommendation statement
Explanatory notes
Detailed guidance notes to help the developer understand the recommendation
Applicability - technology purpose
General | Diagnostic | Monitoring | Screening | Treatment
Applicability -AI
General | Yes | No
Applicability - risk
General | Critical | Serious | Non-serious
Applicability - life cycle stage
General | Pre-clinical | Experimental | Early clinical | Comparative | Surveillance
